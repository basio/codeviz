	Question: How to get the process id to kill a nohup process
		stackoverflow.com/questions/17385794/how-to-get-the-process-id-to-kill-a-nohup-process

	command:

		nohup mrt > meteor.log 2>&1&
		echo $! > save_pid.txt
		kill -9 `cat save_id.txt`

	<or>
		nohup mrt
		ps -ef | grep nohup
		kill -9 <pid>
